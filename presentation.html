The AI Landscape

Understanding the yesterday, today, and tomorrow of AI in service delivery

Yesterday

The chat era

AI as a conversational assistant: useful for research, drafting, and troubleshooting, but still largely passive.

Today

The agentic shift

AI as an active participant: reading tickets, logs, and documentation to classify, diagnose, summarise, and recommend next actions.

Tomorrow

Intelligent operations

AI as an operator within guardrails: testing, validating, correcting, documenting, and escalating only the true edge cases.

The shift is clear: AI is moving from assistant → actor → operator.

THE BUILD FRAMEWORK

Two chassis. Two different jobs.

PROTOTYPE CHASSIS

Fast. Accessible. Designed to sell an idea.

  • Vercel + GitHub — instant deploys, no cloud expertise required
  • OpenRouter for AI — fast model access, no infrastructure setup
  • Accessible to non-technical consultants — not just developers
  • Goal: working demo in days, not weeks
PRODUCTION CHASSIS

Compliant. Reusable. Enterprise-ready.

  • AWS infrastructure — control, security, data residency
  • AWS Bedrock for AI — enterprise-grade, POPIA-compliant
  • Reusable SF + SAP IAS authentication pattern
  • Funded by the next client project — not internal budget

JobFit proved the prototype chassis works and taught us what the production chassis needs to be. Every build after this gets the benefit of that investment.
